Crime Data

This page provides information on Violent Crime and Property Crime in Florida. Statistics are collected at the city level (vs. zip code) and represent average crime rates per capita. For example, a per capita property crime rate of .005 means that one crime occurs per 200 people in an average year.

To navigate the visualization and tables below, hover over the map and you will see additional information when your cursor hovers over a particular zip code. Also notice a few different icons appear in the top left which helps us to navigate the map. + and - are used to zoom in and zoom out. Click on the sideways arrow, then on the icon that appears with two insersecting lines and four arrows. If you then click your mouse and hold it, you can pan left, right, up and down. If you're not sure where the cities are located in the table on the left, you can search for the location of a city using the search bar at the top of she screen. To filter out homes with higher crime rates, use the scalers at the top of the screen. Also, feel free to browse the tables on the left to see the crime rates for particular cities.
